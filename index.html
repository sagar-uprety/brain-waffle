Instructions

Why Mental Health?

One in three adults suffers from mental health issues. However, people often believe mental illness is not a “real” illness or cannot be treated effectively. Mental illnesses are as real as diseases such as heart disease or cancer. But social stigmas and myths stimulate people to believe that there is no such thing as mental illness discouraging the patients from seeking help.

Our Mission

Understanding that mental illness isn't a weakness or a character flaw helps people get help. Our mission is to change the perception of an individual towards mental health, ultimately reducing mental health stigma and realizing the importance of mental health. Our Solution

Our project Brain Waffle provides an immersive VR experience using a 3D brain model to understand the different mental disorders. Users can view the specific brain regions and parts that are affected by certain disorders, their characteristics, and chemical imbalance. This will help people realize that mental illness is real and embrace the people suffering from mental illness.

Using the Application

Please use the "Experience in VR" button above to go to the VR Page.

If your device does not support VR, then you can use it in PC browser as well.
Use and drag your mouse to see the view. Use 'Shift' + drag to move the camera.
Press 'Tab' to start the animation.
Press 'a' key to get the next disease and 'd' key to go back to previous one.
